FAQ Section (Common Objections Preemptively Addressed)
Q: Will I look frozen or overfilled?  A: No. Our providers are trained in “natural results” technique – soft, balanced enhancement. We emphasize maintenance of expression and proportion.


Q: Is it safe for my skin tone?  A: Yes. We treat every skin tone and texture. Our providers use Fitzpatrick-type calibration for lasers and select appropriate products. For melanin-rich skin, we use Nd:Yag 1064 for laser hair removal and conservative settings for all energy-based treatments.


Q: What if I don’t know what I want?  A: That’s exactly what the free consultation is for. Your provider will assess, educate, and recommend – no obligation.


Q: How much will it cost?  A: Exact pricing varies by treatment and location. During your free consultation, you’ll receive a detailed, itemized plan. We offer financing with true 0% APR and up to $50,000 approvals.


Q: Do I tip the provider?  A: No. As medical providers, we do not accept gratuity. Your treatment investment is all-inclusive.


Q: How long does a treatment take?  A: Most treatments are 30–60 minutes. Injectables about 15–30 minutes. Body contouring sessions about 30 minutes. Your provider will give you exact timing during the consult.


Q: What’s the downtime?  A: Many treatments have zero downtime (Botox, fillers, Hydrafacial). Lasers and RF may have 24–48 hours of mild redness. Your provider explains everything in advance.


Q: How many sessions do I need?  A: It depends on the treatment and your goals. Typically, injectables are every 3–4 months, laser hair removal requires 6–8 sessions, body contouring often 3–4 sessions. All explained during your consult.

5. Conversion Optimization Package
A/B Testing Roadmap (Priority Rankings)


Mobile vs. Desktop Optimization Strategies
* Desktop: Long-form copy with scannable subheads, side-by-side testimonials, embedded before/after gallery.
* Mobile: Short paragraphs (max 2–3 sentences), accordion FAQ, swipeable testimonial carousel, sticky CTA bar.
* Load Speed: Compress all images to under 100KB; lazy-load below-fold sections; minify CSS/JS. Target <3s load on 4G.
* Visual Hierarchy: Use Ever/Body’s existing brand palette (clean white, dark green, accent coral). Headlines in bold serif or sans-serif (matching site). Call-to-action buttons in high-contrast accent.

8. Technical Implementation Guide
Tracking Pixel Placement Recommendations
* Facebook Pixel: Place on page load (all traffic) + event for “Book Free Consultation” button click.
* Google Ads Conversion: Fire on confirmation page (consult booked).
* Integrate with Zenoti: Use Zenoti’s tracking pixel for cross-referencing lead source vs. booking source.


CRM Integration Points for Lead Capture
* Form fields: First name, email, phone, location preference, primary concern (dropdown), how they heard about us (dropdown).
* Auto-tagging: Tag CRM entry with “Sales Page – [Traffic Source] – [Concern]” for segmentation.
* Webhook: Upon submission, send lead data to Zenoti booking API to pre-fill intake.


Mobile Responsiveness Requirements
* Viewport meta tag:<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
* Responsive breakpoints: 320px, 480px, 768px, 1024px, 1280px.
* Font sizes: Minimum 16px body text; headings scale down proportionally.
* Images: Use srcset for responsive images; compress to WebP with fallback.
* Buttons: Minimum 48x48px touch target; touch-action: manipulation to prevent zoom.


Page Speed Optimization Checklist


Analytics Setup for Conversion Tracking
* Google Analytics 4: Set up events for “page_view”, “scroll_depth_25/50/75/100”, “cta_click_{CTA_name}”, “form_submission_success”.
* Conversion path: Track landing_page → consultation_booking → booking_confirmation as a funnel.
* Cross-domain tracking: Ensure analytics works between landing page domain and Zenoti booking subdomain.
* UTM parameters: Capture utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_content, utm_term for all inbound traffic.
